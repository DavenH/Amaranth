# Date

2026-07-16

# Context

Cycle v2 needs live red/blue envelope modulation while audio playback and
graphic traversal consume envelopes under different lifecycle and optimization
constraints.

# Decisions

- Share the authoritative envelope preparation and sampling core between audio
  and graphics.
- Do not force traversal graphics through per-voice audio lifecycle machinery.
- Let traversal exploit its complete time-axis rollout, while audio preserves
  loop, release, cursor, realtime, and waveform-boundary requirements.
- Treat dynamic modulation as runtime control and immutable-result publication,
  not repeated persistent graph edits.

# Generalized Rules

- Reuse domain computation, not necessarily consumer lifecycle or orchestration.
- Make shared cores produce complete domain values that specialized consumers
  can process with domain-appropriate optimizations.
- Require semantic parity at shared boundaries while permitting different
  scheduling, continuity, and traversal policies.

# Open Questions

- Choose the exact safe audio adoption boundary and modulation combination rule
  when implementing the dynamic-envelope TDD.
